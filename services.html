SERVICES

We are champions for healthier and safer lives

Our team with many professionals includes the world’s leading public health experts, including doctors, epidemiologists, scientists and managers. Together, we coordinate the world’s response to health emergencies, promote well-being, prevent disease, and expand access to health care. By connecting nations, people and partners to scientific evidence they can rely on, we strive to give everyone an equal chance at a safe and healthy life.

Our Leadership Team

With strong connections between offices,Our organisation works on the front lines in 150+ locations across six regions.Our organisation's Director-General outlines the vision and oversees all our international health work whilst our Regional Directors lead the work of the six Regional Offices, and their country offices. The Regional Directors work closely with the Director-General to implement strategies and programmes across all levels of the Organization.

Member States

Our organisation works with all Member States to support them to achieve the highest standard of health for all people. Our staff working in countries gather data from ministries of health and other sectors on public health issues and provide support to plan, implement and monitor health programmes.

Ensuring uninterrupted service during the COVID-19 pandemic

During the COVID-19 pandemic, uninterrupted access to governements quality standards and support services is critical to helping manufacturers, scientists, healthcare practitioners and regulatory bodies continue protecting public health through the delivery of safe and effective medicines. We are committed to keeping stakeholders informed about the availability of USP Reference Standards, free programs to support the development of vaccines and treatments, how we’re managing any critical material shortages that arise and the offers of discounted educational courses and free webinars to help manufacturers, labs and healthcare practitioners during this global health crisis.

ABOUT US

Dedicated to the well-being of all people and guided by science,Our Organization make global efforts to give everyone,everywhere regular updates about COVID -19.Our organisation's work remains firmly rooted in the basic principles of the right to information for all people, as outlined in our 1948 Constitution. The World Health Assembly is the decision-making body of our organisation and is attended by delegations from all Member States.
